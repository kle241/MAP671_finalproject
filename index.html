Map of Total Revenue in State Government (Avg. 2000-2019)

Full-screen version with detail description

Description

This map is regarding the total revenue in state government from 2000 to 2019. The values of this map are 20 years(`00-`19) average value of total revenue. Given that the total revenue is considered as fiscal capacity, this map demonstrates fiscal capacity of state government. In addition, AK and HI are excluded from this map due to the extreme values in total revenue. Thus, I removed these two states by using the winsorization of STATA.

Program and Database Details

Program: QGIS 3.36.0. Data: [1] Total Revenue: Historical Dataset Fiscal Survey of States from NASBO (2000-2019) and [2] Boundary shp: United States Census Bureau
